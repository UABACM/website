ACM @ UAB Resources

What are these?

Here is a collection of some cool resources. These resources are designed to help you navigate personal projects, UAB CS classes, and CS as a whole. We collected and put them all here based to take away the guesswork of how to prepare for future classes and to present the best version of yourself in the everchanging CS world.
